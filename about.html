Learning Objectives

What you'll learn

  • Describe network fundamentals and build simple LANs
  • Explain the OSI and TCP/IP models
  • Explain IP addressing and subnetting
  • Understand differences between routing protocols
  • Learn about network protocols including VLANs, VPNs, and DNS
  • Develop routing tables for routers to efficiently forward packets
  • Evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of different protocol types
